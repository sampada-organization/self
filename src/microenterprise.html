Micro Enterprise

The microenterprise has proven to be the backbone of economy both at micro and macro level. SELF provides motivational, information as well as managerial inputs to SHGs and JLG’s for undertaking income generating activities and micro enterprises.

Micro enterprises are a driving force of Indian economy. These enterprises not only impact the economy on a large scale but also caters to the needs of the community in the rural and semi – urban parts of the country and also acts as a source of employment for millions in the unprovided part of the country. Keeping in view the importance of micro enterprises and keeping in view its vision SELF is committed to provide congenial environment to such enterprises by not only providing credit for working capital needs but also handholding and providing capacity building trainings to its members.
